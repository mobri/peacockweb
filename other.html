Performance and Coaching

For those of you who would like to experiment, or hone in skills of performance, this is the place for you.

Burlesque Moves workshop

Book yourself a chance to bust the moves, poses and looks of burleque. This workshop is a chance to learn the choreography behind a show and to learn how to express yourself and is perferct for a hen party so the girls can dress up and show off some moves. It is fun, and also a bit of a work out. Foxy will help you with moves that you can incorporate into your style. The level of difficulty is up to you as everything will be taylored to the requirements of the group or individual.

The workshop can be thought over the internet via video confernencing to you at home also.
