Foxy P Cox

The best of Ireland's leading burlesque performances.

The Peacock Parlour is the brain child of Foxy who also does everything necessary or needed from directing, producing, advertising, performing, ticket sales, sound and pretty much everything in-between. This sow is a Burlesque, Cabaret and Variety show, now when we say variety we really mean it, with acts ranging in everything from theatre, performance art and Cirque - Aerialists, Acrobalance and acrobatics, Pole, Hula Hoop, Jugglers, Magicians, Sideshow, fire, sword swallower's, Snake charmers, Burlesque babes and boys, show girls and art installations! No show is ever the same, come be amazed, mesmerized, shocked and turned on! we also show all levels from international performers to Foxy's own students , you will see every different shape size,race creed and colour, absolutely everyone is welcome and celebrated!!

Foxy P. Cox is well known on the Irish scene for infusing the aerial arts with her love of Burlesque, creating stunning acts of gorgeous grace, and winning her a firm place in the hearts of her audience with performances as varied as her eyeshadows. An expert when it comes to vintage style having started her collection at a young age with her grandmother's help, her world opened up in 2009 when the Tassel Club came to Cork. Since then she has worked tirelessly working from the ground up to gain the necessary experience and skill, learning from the best around her. Starting backstage, flyering and on the door, ticket office or as an usherette, wardrobe mistress, make up artist, model, Her hard work has paid off, winning Cork’s ‘Street Style Icon’ in 2011, then teaming with Lisette Kroll and Terri Fierce to win the ‘Best Troupe’ and ‘Audience Choice’ awards at the Dublin Burlesque Fest 2012 to judging it 2013. Winner of Miss Pride Ireland 2013 and guest judge 2014. Headlined the Toulouse Burlesque festival March 2014 before Competing in Miss Burlesque Ireland July 2014 coming in the final 5 then on the judging panel the following year in 2015. She now runs a regular burlesque and cabaret night in cork called The Peacock Parlour in various venues such as The Marlboro Lounge and The City Limits Comedy Club that includes a variety of styles from burlesque,cabaret, drag, pole, aerials, art installations, magicians, sideshow, hula Hooper's, showgirls and everything in between. Watch this space for its guest spots in venues around Ireland soon!

The Peacock Parlour is what we like to call a varitease show, there is a little something for everyone. From Burlesque, Cabaret, Circus to Drag, Aerials, Acrobatics, sideshow, Magicians and much more! It is a magical, wonderfully weird world that will capture your imagination and get your heart pumping. Hosted by Foxy P.Cox, a Cork based international performer. Foxy has headlined burlesque festivals in Toulouse, London and Berlin and specialises in aerial striptease. It’s a show not to be missed.
