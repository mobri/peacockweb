The Peacock Parlour Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will explain how we use the personal data we collect from you when you sign up with us or provide us with any personal data.

Topics:

  • What data do we collect?
  • How do we collect your data?
  • How will we use your data?
  • How do we store your data?

    • What data do we collect?

    If you sign up to the mailing list we collect the data you provide in the form. This at least includes email address which will be stored on mailchimp. Other form signups for performers are handled by Google Cloud where contact information such as email and phone number are stored for future contact with performers.

    How do we collect your data?

    You directly provide us with most of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you voluntarily complete a form or provide feedback on any of our social media or via email. Social Media services data policies apply on those platforms.

    How will we use your data?

    For the expressed purposes stated in the form. To contact you about performances and shows.

    How do we store your data?

    We securely store your data at on Google Cloud with 2 factor authentication and limited access to the shows producers only. Email list subscriptions are stored on mailchimp. We will keep your data until such time as you ask us to delete that data which you can do by clicking 'unsubscribe' on the emails you recieve.

    What are your data protection rights?

    The Peacock Parlour would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every person who has provided us with data via our forms is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • The right to erasure – You have the right to request we erase your personal data.

    • How to contact us

    If you have any questions about our privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.
